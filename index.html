# NVIDIA Stock Performance Analysis (1999–2026) An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 26 years of NVIDIA Corporation (NASDAQ: NVDA) stock performance — from its 1999 IPO to the Generative AI boom of 2023–2026. ![NVIDIA Dashboard](dashboard-screenshot.png) ## 📊 Overview This dashboard tracks NVIDIA's transformation from a niche gaming GPU company to a $500B+ AI computing giant, using historical price, market cap, revenue, and technical indicator data across five distinct business eras. ## 🔑 Key Insights - **All-time high close price:** $20.73 - **Peak market capitalization:** $507.71B - **7,079 trading days** analyzed (1999–2026) - **Generative AI Era (2023–2026)** generated an average quarterly revenue of $17.33B — over 9x higher than the Data Center Growth era - RSI-14 momentum indicator shows a clear bullish shift starting in 2023, aligned with the AI boom ## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used - **Power BI Desktop** — dashboard design and data modeling - **DAX** — custom measures for KPI cards (max/min price, formatted market cap, latest close price) - **Power Query** — data cleaning and date/era transformations - **Data visualization** — line charts, bar charts, KPI cards, custom sort orders, and axis filtering ## 📈 Dashboard Pages / Visuals | Visual | Insight | |--------|---------| | KPI Cards | All-time high price, max market cap, total trading days, average RSI | | Yearly Avg Close Price (1999–2026) | Long-term price trend showing explosive growth post-2023 | | Avg Quarterly Revenue by Era | Revenue comparison across NVIDIA's 5 business eras | | Yearly Avg RSI-14 (2020–2026) | Momentum indicator highlighting the AI-era bull run | | Avg Close Price by Month | Seasonal price pattern across calendar months | ## 📂 Dataset - **Source:** [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com) — NVIDIA historical stock dataset (1999–2026) - **Rows:** 7,079 daily trading records - **Columns:** Date, Open, High, Low, Close, Volume, Market Cap, Quarterly Revenue, SMA (20/50/200), RSI-14, Era, Stock Split, Key Events ## 🚀 How to Use 1. Download `NVIDIA_Dashboard.pbix` from this repository 2. Open in Power BI Desktop 3.
